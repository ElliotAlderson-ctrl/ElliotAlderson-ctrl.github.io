18 Landseer Street

Stay Put.

You decide to wait and see if your parents come back to the house, you sit and wait by the window.
Minutes seem like hours and hours seem like days.
Eventually it reaches nightfall. Your family haven't turned up and you haven't heard from them.
You get up from the window and go to the kitchen to make some food.
You turn the light on and you're sat there at the table with your food. A few minutes pass and you begin to hear some strange noises outside.
You go to the window and open the curtains. Seperated only by a pane of double glazing is roughly 15 zombies, they mustve seen the light...
They batter the window and smash it. You're helpless, you try and fend them off, but it's too much.
You're Dead!
