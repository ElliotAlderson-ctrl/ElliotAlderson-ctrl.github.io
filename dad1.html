Going to look for Dad.

You set off to find your Dad, you notice more and more bodies the closer you head towards the school.
The blaze you have seen earlier is getting closer and closer until you realise… The school is what is on fire…
You beat the side of your door in anger there is nothing left but a fiery blaze. There is now only one option, meet your friends in Belfast…
You text your friends and inform them of your whereabouts. They’re thrilled to hear you’re alive.
