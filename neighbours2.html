18 Landseer Street

A walk around the block.

You grab all the supplies you can hold amounting to 5 litres of water and two days’ worth of food.
You then make your way to your car get in and turn the key... The car doesn’t start.
Getting out and checking under the bonnet you notice your starter motor is missing and without it you cannot start your car.
You remember your neighbour up the road has the exact same model of car as you.

Grab the necessary tools and make your way to your neighbour’s house
