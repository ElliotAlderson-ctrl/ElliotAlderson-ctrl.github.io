18 Landseer Street

What is that?

Before going outside, you have another look around.
Checking outside the front of your house nothing has changed you can still see the smoke in the distance coming from Bangor town centre.
You then go and check the back garden, all seems clear, but wait…

There’s someone standing in the corner of the back garden…
You cannot see their face, but their clothing is in tattered and bloody.
You stand and watch the person for a few minutes.
You see your neighbours cat approach them. What you see next is nothing short of grotesque.
They grab the cat and sink its teeth into its neck. It’s a zombie. What should you do?

Go out and fight the zombie?
