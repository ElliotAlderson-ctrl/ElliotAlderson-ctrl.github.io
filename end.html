18 Landseer Street

Finding your friends.

You reach your friend’s house and knock the door.
You see a lot of movement behind the frosted glass and what seems like forever the door finally opens.
You are met with the barrel of a handgun, it’s your best friend Phil. He asks “are you bitten, have you any bites”
you try and reason with him to let you in, but he is having none of it.
You are forced to strip naked so they can check for bites.
You’re all clear, they let you enter and the second you hear the door lock you breakdown as you begin to process the horror this day has been.

THE END
