Murder on the Dancefloor.

Your aim is perfect, the zombie clatters to the ground, but it is still alive.
You continue to attack the head until finally the zombie stops moving.
You’re covered in blood, but you collect yourself and remind yourself they aren’t the person they once were,
just a vicious body craving one thing and one thing only… FLESH.
You go back inside and grab all the supplies you gathered amounting to 5 litres of water and two days’ worth of food.
You then make your way to your car get in and turn the key... The car doesn’t start.
Getting out and checking under the bonnet you notice your starter motor is missing and without it you cannot start your car.
You remember your neighbour up the road has the exact same model of car as you.
