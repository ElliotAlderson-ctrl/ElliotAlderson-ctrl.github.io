18 Landseer Street

Finding Mum.

You set off and drive cautiously as your car is obviously making a noticeable amount of noise.
This is the first time you’ve seen the extend of the damage to your local estate.
You notice people peeking out of their blinds as you drive past, some even trying to signal you.
There’s no time you need to find your parents. The closer you get towards the nursing home you see a slew
of bodies in the streets along with a few zombies who try and chase the car, but they give up relatively easy.
About a mile from the nursing home, you have the bright idea to turn your engine off and let the car roll the
rest of way to attract less attention. Approaching the nursing home, you notice there is a hoard of zombies,
you now begin to accept that your mother is gone. You can’t stop and process it there’s no time.
Suddenly there is a radio transmission being broadcasted over your favourite station. “Bangor has been completely
overrun with zombies, AVOID AT ALL COSTS”.
You begin to sob behind the wheel and try and decide whether or not to go looking for your dad.
